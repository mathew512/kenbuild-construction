About Us

KenBuild Construction Ltd is a leading construction and engineering company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. For over 15 years, we have delivered residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects with precision, craftsmanship, and integrity.

Our experienced team of architects, engineers, and project managers combines modern technology with sustainable building practices to achieve lasting results. From concept design to final hand‑over, we focus on safety, structural durability, and client satisfaction.

Guided by our vision to shape Kenya’s urban landscape responsibly, KenBuild embraces innovation, environmental stewardship, and transparent communication — ensuring that every project we deliver contributes to a stronger, greener nation.
